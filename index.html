What I Fix

Common issues solved with professional diagnostics and high-quality cells.

Blinking Red Light

Flashing Red LED

Battery detected but won’t charge. This is usually due to cell imbalance or a single weak cell. I reset the board and balance the cells to make it like new.

No Lights / Dead

Completely unresponsive packs that refuse to wake up. I perform deep diagnostics to find the break and restore functionality safely.

Performance Rebuilds

Full cell replacement using high-discharge premium cells. Get better runtime and power than the original pack at a lower price point.

Choose Your Cells

Tested Grade A Cells

Premium New Cells

Brand new high-discharge cells (Samsung, Sony, or LG). Maximum power output, longest lifespan, and best thermal performance.

"Can you just reuse my old cells?"

Technically possible, but not recommended. Mixed or worn cells often lead to the charger stopping at 2-3 bars. For full capacity and safety, cell replacement is always the best path.
